Manalo Medical Clinic – Patient Functional Overview

User Role: Patient / Client

Purpose: Enable patients to register, manage their profile, book appointments, track their consultation history, communicate with the clinic, and receive notifications—all online.

Patient Capabilities / Functions
1. Registration & Profile Management

Create an account with email and password

Provide personal information: full name, gender, date of birth, address, contact number

Optional medical info: blood type, allergies, existing conditions, emergency contact

Update or edit profile anytime

Secure login and logout

2. Appointment Booking

Multi-step guided booking process:

Personal Info (auto-filled from profile)

Schedule Selection (calendar with available days/times)

Consultation Details (type, reason, preferred doctor)

Review & Submit

Real-time availability check and booking limits per time slot

Status tracking: Pending → Approved / Rejected → Completed / Cancelled

3. Appointment History & Status

View upcoming appointments with date, time, doctor, and status

Filter by date range or status

Review past appointments and consultation summaries

4. Notifications

Receive real-time in-system notifications for:

Appointment approval or rejection

Upcoming appointment reminders

Status updates and changes

Optional email notifications

5. Live Chat & AI Assistant

Floating chat widget for real-time communication

AI assistant handles:

Booking, rescheduling, and cancelling appointments

Checking appointment status

Providing clinic hours, services, and directions

Answering common internal medicine FAQs

Escalation to secretary if inquiry is sensitive or AI confidence is low

6. Clinic Locator Map

Embedded interactive map showing:

Clinic name, address, contact number, schedule

“Get Directions” button linking to Google Maps

Mini-map accessible in patient dashboard

Summary of Patient Modules
Module	Key Functions
Registration & Profile	Create & edit account, manage personal & medical info
Appointment Booking	Book, review, and submit appointments
Appointment History & Status	View past and upcoming appointments, filter by date/status
Notifications	Receive real-time updates and reminders
Live Chat & AI Assistant	Ask questions, manage appointments, communicate with staff
Clinic Locator Map	View location, get directions, see clinic hours
